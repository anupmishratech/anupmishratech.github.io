About

The Superconductors is an Indian semi-classical/pop Band. The Band was founded by singer Anup Mishra in 2014, when he met other music enthusiasts like Brad Harris(Drums), Luke Fennewald(Bass), Yifei Lou(Guitar), and Jeremiah Rittel(Clarinet/Flute/Saxophone). The band performed in the University of Missouri India Nite 2014 as it's debut performance and has continued to perform in all the India Nites events since then. In the last few years of it's existence, many skilled musicians have contributed to the band, such as, Eric Bohle(Bass), Joe Weidinger(Guitar/Piano), Xiaoxiao Du(Violin), Zack Garrett(Bass), Zack Clark(Bass), and Naman Arora (Drums). We appreciate the constant support of the Indian Community in Columbia, Missouri.
